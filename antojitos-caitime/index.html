About Antojitos Caitime

Antojitos Caitime is importing tradition to San Jose, serving up Mexican eats to the streets. That, and so much more than that. Because true, antojitos usually means snacks that are tangentially related to more heartier Mexican meals, but at Antojitos Caitime, they've broadened the term to include other street foods too. Point is, you're able to indulge your cravings however you want, in a full bodied menu built to satisfy. At Antojitos Caitime, they're committed to using the freshest quality ingredients in every hand crafted meal. Homemade salsas are made on the daily, and meats come hot off the grill right when you order 'em. And here, you're getting traditional south of the border flavors, and more than just tacos and burritos. From tamales and their signature torta ahogada drenched in red chili sauce to ceviche with the tenderest of shrimp morsels, you're getting all the Mexican food you can handle. But that's not all. Antojitos Caitime features a menu of vegetarian favorites and rotating specialties, including chicken nuggets, egg rolls, loaded fries and pizza. Basically what we're saying is, you've got options, and Antojitos Caitime is available to deliver. Literally. Book 'em to get all the snacks and meals delivered at your next event, or find 'em cruising the streets of Sunnyvale and beyond.
