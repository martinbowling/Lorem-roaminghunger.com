About Munchies Food Truck

Munchies Food Truck is offering up innovative takes on comfort food classics. You'll find all your old favorites amped up with new flavors. From Tex-Mex to barbecue to classic feel-good fare like mac & cheese, this Dallas-based business is going for gold with homey, soul-warming fare. They've got both a trailer and a large truck stocked with retro video games for guests to enjoy, both sporting quiet generators, making 'em well suited for both small, intimate get-togethers and large festivals. Feel like Mexican? Go for some nachos, tacos, or the famous nacho fries if you want to double down on the decadence. Or, if you prefer, sink your teeth into a bacon cheeseburger or a barbecue pulled pork sandwich. Love mac 'n cheese? Try it pressed into a panini. Fancy an old-fashioned PB&J? Have it dipped in batter and then deep-fried. Plus, at every event they cater, Munchies serves up their signature chicken and pork both packed with smoky flavor. Drooling yet? Then book Munches Food Truck for your next get-together. MUST TRY Wild Knight Panini — Cheddar, provolone, mac & cheese, and pulled pork
