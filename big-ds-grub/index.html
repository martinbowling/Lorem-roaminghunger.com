About Big D's Grub

Big D's Grub is cookin' up a whole lotta grub for native New Yorkers. In fact, it's an entire food truck's worth of good grub, made gourmet using only the finest ingredients around. Plus, it's hearty enough to fully curb your hunger on the curb. If there's anyone that knows what native New Yorkers crave, it's Big D, aka Dennis. He grew up in what's considered the culinary mecca in Queens, surrounded by a bunch of good food at family gatherings. That's just what you'll find at Big D's Grub, food made with love and inspired by the bold flavors of Guyana, the American South and China. Start your day off right with a hearty breakfast meal, including a big ol' breakfast burrito and a Spanish omelette stuffed with spam. Need a midday pick me up? Not to worry; Big D's Grub's got tacos for days, along with hearty rice platters featuring the exotic flavors of bulgogi beef over a bed of rice with kimchi. Not to mention his homemade pork and chive dumplings served with sriracha or ginger sauce. Mouth watering yet? Good. You'll find the cure at Big D's Grub in New York, NY or you can have Big D himself out to cater your next event. Trust us. He won't disappoint.
