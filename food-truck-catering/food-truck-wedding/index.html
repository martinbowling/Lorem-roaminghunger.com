Food Truck Wedding Catering

We’re your culinary matchmaker. At Roaming Hunger, our technology and expertise pairs you with your perfect catering experience in 45 days or less.
  • Wedding Catering
  • Frequently Asked Questions

We Are The Food Truck Experts

Finding a great wedding caterer is stressful - let our experienced team do the work so you don't have to. Once hired, your personal food truck coordinator will gather the best options available and help you select the caterer and menu that's right for you. Whether you want a unique food experience for your wedding reception, rehearsal dinner, late night snack or next day brunch, we are at your service.

Give Us the Details

If you haven’t already, click “Get Started” to tell us about your wedding. After a phone call with your dedicated catering coordinator, we’ll prepare a quote based on the food truck experience you’re envisioning.

We Conduct the Ultimate Search

When you’re ready to move forward, retain our services with a signed service agreement and $250 retainer that goes toward your catering. We’ll reach out to every local food truck that’s a good fit for you and get their menus.

Go Forth and Taste Test

After you’ve narrowed down the options to two or three favorites, we’ll schedule a tasting to try each food truck. Every tasting booked through Roaming Hunger is on us - yum!

Book your Favorite(s)

Within 45 days, we’ll lock in your date, caterer(s), and menu with a 50% deposit. Two weeks before your event, we’ll collect the remaining balance and finalize all event details.

Enjoy Your Big Day

Roaming Hunger is with you every step of the way. If there are any surprises, we’ll find a replacement caterer. We also guarantee every single catering with the 100% Roaming Hunger Service Guarantee.

Why Work With Us?

Every food truck catering is protected by the 100% Roaming Hunger Service Guarantee.

A Menu That Fits You

Curate the right cuisine for you and your guests. Whether you're looking for sophisticated upscale fare or casual comfort food, we'll help you navigate our thousands of options. We will also work with the caterer to modify the menu you love to meet any dietary requirements.

Service Guarantee

Get the fantastic service you deserve on your wedding day. We have established relationships with reliable food trucks so you can book with confidence, and we hold caterer payment in escrow until after the event as an added guarantee. If something does go wrong, we will find a way to make it right.

Peace of Mind

Be sure that your food truck is serving safely and legally at your wedding. We secure all the necessary paperwork, including contracts, W9s, certificates of insurance, and health and business permits. We are catering professionals and we expect every vendor we book to be professional too.

Flexible Service Styles

We'll customize the experience to fit your needs. Whether you want guests to mingle in line at the food truck, self serve with a traditional buffet, or interact at food stations, many of our 10,000+ catering vendors offer a variety of ways to engage your guests. Just ask.
