About Uptown Food Truck

Uptown Food Truck is classing up downtown classics in Atlanta, GA with a high-class sheen. And every bit of that sheen is edible. From thickly-brushed honey mustard wings to sauce-drenched polish sausage to gourmet burgers too, there's a bevy of thick eats stuffed on-board, just waiting to make your acquaintance. So don't keep 'em waiting because they’re all so good. Uptown Food Truck serves Atlanta and all of the surrounding areas. No matter where they are, it's the perfect spot to pause your day and find your bliss in all its shiny, scrumptious glory. Stop by Uptown Food Truck to grab all this fanciness, or elevate your friends' expectations at your next big party by making use of their professional catering. If you’re craving something you don’t see on the menu, have no fear. Uptown Food Truck provides a selection of special menus and customizable catering packages to fit your needs, whatever they are. Book Uptown Food Truck soon because they will class up any wedding, party, or corporate event, even as your guests turn into savages.
