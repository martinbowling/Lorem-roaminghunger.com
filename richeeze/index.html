About Richeeze

Richeeze, a Los Angeles-based truck, is changing the way we think about the grilled cheese sandwich. By incorporating the highest quality meats and cheeses as well as talent from the area’s best chefs, Richeeze is setting a new standard for gourmet on the go. From the lobster mac (featuring chunks of tender lobster and homemade mac & cheese) to "Ally's Special" (stuffed with mushrooms, melted mozzarella and basil pesto), rest assured there's a cheesy meal for everyone here. The deep fried mac 'n' cheese nuggets aren't too shabby either; same goes for the cheese fries. Honestly, there are no wrong choices here, just cheesy ones. So why wait? Find Richeeze for the richest grilled cheese sammies in town, or hire 'em to cater your next event in LA. MUST TRY Jalapeño Grilled Cheese — Jalapeños melted together with oh-so-much cheese and grilled onions. Pair it with tomato soup, because obviously.
