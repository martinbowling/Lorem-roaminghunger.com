About G's Taco Spot on Wheels

It's all about hearty, Mexican cooking at G's Taco Spot on Wheels. The LA-based truck is slinging traditional recipes alongside authentic spices and seasonings, providing a tasty backbone for all of their dishes. Craving Mexican food for breakfast? Us too, to be honest, and G's Taco Spot delivers on all fronts, serving up big ol' steak and egg burritos alongside Mexi-French toast and hearty breakfast quesadillas. How about tacos for lunch? They've got a ton of tasty meats on board too, from the aptly named S&M tacos with shrimp and meaty carne asada, to the Chicken Affair drizzled in chipotle ranch. For all these steamy options and more, find G's Taco Spot on Wheels in LA, or have 'em out to cater your next event.
