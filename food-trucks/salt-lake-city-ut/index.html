Snow can't stop Salt Lake City's love of food! Utah is now home to the greatest snow on earth AND some of the greatest food trucks, too. The snowy city has year-long farmers markets like the SLC Winter Market, where food trucks love to hang out, as well as many curbside spots where the vendors serve up their delicious dishes. Local favorite Chow Truck will be anywhere from the Soho Food Park to the Urban Flea market, dishing out Asian fusion favorites like pineapple-ginger pork sliders, and there are also "Food Truck League" weekly gatherings at Sugarmont Plaza on Mondays over the summer, with live music and a festival atmosphere. The league is dedicated to serving Salt Lake the best gourmet food on wheels, and lets food trucks join for free!
