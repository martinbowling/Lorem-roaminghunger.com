More Trucks
Jacksonville is home to white beaches, miles of river walk, and - most importantly - food trucks! Jax locals can get their street food fix at Downtown's Art Walk every first Wednesday of the month, or Saturday mornings at the Riverside Arts Market. Don't miss Travel Channel Winner Gourmet Aviator, which serves bacon-wrapped dates from a silver jet-inspired truck. Jax also shows off its culinary accomplishments at GastroFest at Hemming Park, with several trucks in attendance. From the Funkadelic truck's beet chips to Chew Chew's lobster corn dogs, there's never a dull taste in Jacksonville.
