Originally called Gastown, Vancouver is getting back to its roots. With around 40 trucks cruising the streets of this heavily populated city, everyone's getting their fair share of fare. The trucks head downtown to get the most action, where business people and night clubbers alike feast under the sun and stars on anything from Korean tacos to sushi cones. Don't miss the delicious Japadog truck and their creatively-topped hot dogs! With a film industry that rivals Los Angeles' no wonder Vancouver is catching on to the food truck frenzy too.
