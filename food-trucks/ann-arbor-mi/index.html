More Trucks
Ann Arbor's Wolverines are certainly ravenous for street food. Luckily this college town doesn't have to look to hard to find its favorite trucks - Mark's Carts between First and Ashley has seven permanent food carts on-site adjacent to a beer garden. Favorite dishes include Thai Spicy Potato and Peas from Great Grilled Sandwiches, and Chicken Pot Stickers or Deep Fried Jalapeno Dumplings from Mei Mei's. Trucks like Shimmy Shack and San Street roam more freely, and if you're lucky you'll find them at the Wednesday evening food truck rally at the farmer's market.
