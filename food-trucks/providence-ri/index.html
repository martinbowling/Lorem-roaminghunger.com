More Trucks
Little Rhody packs big taste for its size. Recently named one of America's top foodie cities, Providence is no stranger to the food truck revolution. The Food Truck Market at Kennedy Plaza has been serving hot meals since 2010, five days a week. Want a more scenic lunch? Stop by Rhode Island's only beachfront food truck park - Ocean State - at 50 Niantic Ave. Nibble a Brazilian dish from Lady Copacabana on the beach, or wait only two minutes for a whole pizza from The Gilded Tomato. Providence took on Boston in the Providence vs. Boston Food Truck showdown, with its favorite truck Shuckin' Truck dishing out mago lobster tacos, and it's proved it can go toe-to-toe with any city out there!
