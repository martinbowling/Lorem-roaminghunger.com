More Trucks
Boston's a college town, and there are almost as many food trucks as there are ounces in the favorite undergrad beverage. With nearly 40 food trucks in the area, students and townies alike are never at a loss for good food.

Suited business people battle the Occupiers in the Financial District to fuel up at Milk & Kilby while the studious bookworms take a break outside the Boston Public Library. Wherever there are hungry people, you can bet there's a truck to feed them in the Bean.

Roxy's Grilled Cheese is on the forefront of cheesy innovation while the famed Bon Me Truck serves up refreshing Vietnamese fare.

The trucks line up to feed the hungry BU Terriers on Commonwealth and gather on Boylston to give the Emerson hipsters some food for thought. The food truck trend is a new movement in an old town and they're sure to join the greats in the next gen's history books. Be a part of history. Click on the live map above to find your next meal!
