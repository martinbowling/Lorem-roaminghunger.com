More Trucks
Kansas City, often referred to as the heart of America, is stealing the hearts of foodies everywhere. The rapidly expanding food truck industry in this Midwestern city is bringing residents tasty treats in the streets. One KC delicacy is their famous Kansas City style BBQ, epitomized in the KC BBQ Truck's scrumptious smoked & grilled meats. By the time you've had your fill of BBQ you'll probably find yourself slipping into a food-coma but don't worry, the CoffeeCakeKC truck is at your service! Every single bean of coffee served from the truck is roasted and brewed by the owner and you can pair the bold coffee with an item from the bakery (bananas foster cupcake, maybe?) Many KC residents make this a daily morning stop, and they never get bored because the menu changes weekly. If you're ready to fall in love with the food trucks in the heart of America, click on our live map!
