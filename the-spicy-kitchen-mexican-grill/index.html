Meet Roxanne & Richard

It's hard to choose one dish. Nachos are popular, but I would say the Mexican Pizza has quickly become one of my favorites.

What is the story behind your business' name?

We cook spicy food on our little kitchen on wheels. Made to order to satisfy all palates.

About The Spicy Kitchen Mexican Grill

Roxanne and Richard wanted to share their heritage with others via food. Like many small business owners, they turned this simple passion into a booming business. The Spicy Kitchen Mexican Grill is now wowing Denver, CO with its wide array of tasty cuisine made from recipes passed down for generations. So, come spice up your life with this kitchen’s delicious fusion of Mexican and American cuisine. Whether you swing by their food truck or catch 'em catering an event, the eclectic menu is well worth sampling. Using heirloom family recipes and the freshest and finest ingredients, this duo prepares an authentic selection of tacos, tortas, burgers, and much more, all gourmet and made from scratch. Their skillful bakers also present a decadent spread of cakes and desserts, including cake pops and fried ice cream. These zesty specialties will fire up your appetite all day long and during any occasion; breakfast, lunch or dinner, birthdays, holidays, or weddings. So, what are you waiting for? Book The Spicy Kitchen for your next special event and entice your guests with fun and flavorful times. MUST TRY Mexican Pizza — A crispy corn tortilla smothered in beans, sauce, and cheese plus your choice of meat to top it all off.
