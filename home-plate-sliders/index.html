About Home Plate Sliders

It started with a dream to combine America’s pastime and America’s favorite food. After the pieces finally fell together, and a long winter of hard work, Home Plate Sliders was born. Fueled by a passion for sports and fulfilled taste buds, this slider-servin’ restaurant on wheels rolls through the great city of Portland, Oregon. Its mission: to bring some of the greatest things in life to some of the greatest people who live it. It goes without saying that sliders should be the focal point of your visit to the baseball-themed truck—consider the Volcano with teriyaki sauce, pineapple, and lettuce, or the Yankee with pastrami and Swiss—, but don't ignore the rest of the goodies spread across the menu, like the Texas toast grilled cheese or their spiral-cut potato on a stick. Whatever you choose should treat you right, so do yourself a favor and book Home Plate Sliders to cater your next event. MUST TRY The Ranger — BBQ sauce, cheddar, and crispy fried onions
