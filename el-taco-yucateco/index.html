About El Taco Yucateco

The flavors of the Yucatan are on the menu at El Taco Yucateco, a Portland, OR-based taco truck. Whether it’s tortas, tacos, rice bowls, or burritos, Yucateco is able to bring the Mayan-style fire. Choose from either grilled chicken, beef, or pork, and do them up however you want. And because this is the Yucatan we’re talking about, you better believe there are some pickled red onions in the mix. Bring the power of the El Taco Yucateco to your next event, and book them to cater it. MUST TRY Panuchos — Homemade tortillas, shredded chicken, cabbage, tomatoes, avocado, and pickled red onions
